The Hub

Singapore known for its modern skyscrapers and vibrant man-made landmarks. It’s one of the most popular destinations for those travelling oversea, whether it’s for a layover or a fully planned holiday. It connects people all over the world.

Gardens By the Bay

An attraction for all. With multiple gardens to explore the fun never ends.

  • Cloud Forest – Experience a cooled sensation with exotic plant species from all over the world.
  • Floral Fantasy – Walk through an amazing assort of flowers.
  • Flower Dome – Explore the world of spring in floral gardens.
  • Supertree Observatory – With an indoor and open rooftop, view the entirety of the gardens from the highest spot.
  • OCBC Skyway – Walk across the 22m skyway to view the 12 giant man-made tree gardens.
  • Prop Nex Family Zone and Activity Garden – Find a playground that brings out the young hearts of everyone.
