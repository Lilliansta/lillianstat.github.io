Down Under

Tasmania Australia is known for its unique fauna and flora, with its vast range of forests, mountains and rivers. It produces the freshest fruits and vegetables along with high quality meat, especially beef.

Cradle Mountain

Located in the Central Highlands region of Tasmania, Australia, it rests at 1,545 metres above sea level. It has gorgeous views and trails that encounter visitors everyday alongside native Australian wildlife such as wallabies, wombats and echidnas.

Popular Trails

  • Dove Lake Circuit – 5.8 Km, approximately 1 hour and 38 minutes.
  • Enchanted Walk – 1.1 km, approximately 20 minutes.
  • Cradle Valley Boardwalk – 5 km, one way.
  • King Billy Walk – 1.6 km, approximately 45 minutes.
  • Ronny Creek to Dove Lake Circuit – approximately 10.8 km, 2 hours 57 minutes.
